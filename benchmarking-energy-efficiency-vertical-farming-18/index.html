Benchmarking Energy Efficiency in Vertical Farming: Status and Prospects

25 May 2026 1 min read
Soru & Cevap
About this article: This paper establishes systematic energy benchmarks for vertical farming operations, comparing current best-practice installations (10–18 kWh/kg) against theoretical technical optima (3.1–7.4 kWh/kg) across lighting, HVAC, and auxiliary systems. Read this if you want a quantitative baseline for evaluating energy performance in a vertical farm and understanding where the largest efficiency gaps exist.
